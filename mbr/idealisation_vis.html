Idealizing the real world systems

Here the details unnecessary for solving the problem at hand are stripped off, simplified approximations of the real world are use. .


  • Details like colour, material particulars, temparture etc are ignored..

  • We consider the effect of medium of uniform density on the oscillating bob.

  • We update the model to include drag force acting on the person in presence of the medium.

  • The drag force is proportional to the crosssectional area perpendiculum to motion of the body of the person on the swing and the velocity of the bob.
  • Thus the object is approximated as spherical sphere of definite radius.
