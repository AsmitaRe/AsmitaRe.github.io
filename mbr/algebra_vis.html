The viscous damping force is proportional to,
$\hskip 2em$ the speed $v$ and opposes the motion.
$\hskip 2em$ the density of the medium $\rho _{m}$,
$\hskip 2em$ the cross section area of the bob $A_{b}$
, $ F_{d} \propto \rho_{m}*A_{b}*v^{2}$,
$\hskip 2em$ $\Rightarrow F_{d} = C_{v}*\rho _{m}*A_{b}*v^{2} $

The general equation of motion is,
$ \Sigma_{i} \tau_{i} = I\alpha = mL^{2}\frac{d^{2}\theta}{dt^{2}} \hskip 15em$ ..eq(2)

Equation of motion of the pendulum with the torque provided by gravitational, buoyant force and drag force is,
$$ mL\frac{d^{2}\theta }{dt^{2}} + mg_{e}sin\theta - \rho _{m}V_{bob}g_{e} + C_{m}\rho_{m}A_{b}v^{2} = 0 $$
