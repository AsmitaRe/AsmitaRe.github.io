Let us model the motion of the bob in the presence of gravitational forces acting on it.
$ F_{g} = - mg $

The cosine component of the gravitational force is balanced by the tension in the string. While the sine component provides the necessary restoring force that sets the pendulum in motion.
Net Torque, $\hskip 1 em \tau = -mg_{e}Lsin\theta \hskip 10em$ ..eq(1)

The general equation of motion is,
$\tau = I\alpha = mL^{2}\frac{d^{2}\theta}{dt^{2}} \hskip 15em$ ..eq(2)
Equation of motion of the pendulum after insertaing the details of the system is,
$$ mL\frac{d^{2}\theta }{dt^{2}} + mg_{e}sin\theta = 0$$
